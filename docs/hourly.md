A private, real-time visual diary.
Here is how the features function in a dual-user setup:

## 🕒 Shared Hourly Timeline

- Side-by-Side Reality: The app displays your 2-to-4-second video directly next to your partner's clip for that exact hour.
- Instant Comparison: You see exactly what each other is doing at 1:00 PM, 2:00 PM, and throughout the day without asking.
- No Faking It: Because neither person can upload from the camera roll, you both see raw, unedited glimpses of each other's actual day.

## 🎬 A Combined Daily Vlog

- Dual-Perspective Stitching: At the end of the day, the app automatically merges both of your snippets into one chronological video.
- A Shared Memory: The final export creates a unique "day-in-the-life" movie told from two different viewpoints simultaneously.

## 💬 Intimate Micro-Interactions

- Private Dialogue: The group chat and video reactions become a dedicated, private communication channel just for the two of you.
- Contextual Comments: You can leave reactions or comments directly on specific hourly clips, keeping conversations tied to the exact moment they happened.
